Maternal Mortality

The maternal mortality ratio ("MMR") measures the number of deaths per 100,000 live births.
The MMR is one tool to evaluate a country's progress in protecting childbearing women.
Use the interactive graph below to see how countries compare with each other,
and how they have improved and declined in the last three decades.
Countries are ranked by MMR.


Select an option from the dropdown menu.
Hover and click on country names or lines to highlight.
Hover over dots for more information.

1 = Lowest MMR.
